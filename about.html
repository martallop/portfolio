ABOUT

 

Originally from Spain, I’ve lived in South Africa, USA and France. I am currently living and working in New York, building my career as a visual designer.

I have always been drawn to handcrafted projects and illustration. The attention to detail and thoughtfulness behind the craftsmanship is what originally attracted me to this industry. As I’ve learned more, my world of design has expanded past style, and now I am especially passionate about bringing a brand to life through its visual identity and language.
